Welcome to Weather Map service

Hello!
This is Weather Map service. It will help you to get information about weather in different places across Earth.
To get temperature, humidity and other parameters just click on one of existing places over the map. If there are no markers near you just tap on map and you will be prompted to create new places. Then just fill in name and press save.
You will see new marker and be able to click on it! So simple!
